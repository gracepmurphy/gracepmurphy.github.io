AdvEGGture Report Remix

As a child, scrambled eggs were your delight,

But now you can't stomach them, try as you might.

You know eggs are easy protein to get,

But their texture now makes your stomach upset.

You hoped to change your mind while in England,

At Grandma Marina's house, you would attend.

She's sweet as can be and wants you well-fed,

But last time you got sick, and spent the day in bed.

Beef pot pie and TV went down just fine,

But breakfast the next day was a hill to climb.

Bacon and toast were lovely to see,

But runny eggs were not your cup of tea.

You had a choice to make, two paths ahead,

Refuse the food

Or grow up instead.

You took a bite and it was just so bad,

Like eating mucus, it made you gag.

You chewed and chewed and finally swallowed it down,

Your boyfriend was proud, he winked with no frown.

He swapped plates, so it looked like it was his own,

You took the adventure, and seeds of hope were sown.

Maybe with time, eggs won't be so bad,

You'll try them again, and won't be so sad.
